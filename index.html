Our Products

Battery Sprayer

This battery-operated agricultural sprayer is a reliable and convenient tool designed for efficient spraying of pesticides, herbicides, and liquid fertilizers. With its rechargeable battery system, it eliminates the need for manual pumping, allowing continuous and uniform spraying with minimal effort. The sprayer features a high-capacity tank, adjustable nozzle, and a comfortable back strap for easy portability. Ideal for use in farms, gardens, and plantations, this sprayer ensures better crop protection and saves time and labor for farmers.

Fertilizer Seeder

This manual seed drill is a simple and efficient tool designed for accurate and quick sowing of seeds in small-scale farms and garden plots. Its rotating drum with evenly spaced spikes ensures uniform seed placement and proper spacing, helping improve germination rates and reduce seed wastage. The tool is lightweight, easy to push, and does not require fuel or electricity, making it an eco-friendly and cost-effective solution for farmers. Ideal for sowing crops like paddy, pulses, and vegetables, this seed drill boosts planting efficiency while reducing manual effort.

Cow Lifting Machine

This cow lifting machine is a sturdy and practical device designed to help safely lift weak, injured, or post-delivery cattle with minimal stress. Built with a strong metal frame and fitted with a heavy-duty harness, it provides full-body support to the animal during lifting and standing. The machine is equipped with smooth-rolling wheels for easy movement within the farm and features a manual or mechanical lifting system for controlled operation. Ideal for veterinary care, recovery assistance, and animal handling, this equipment is a reliable solution for ensuring cattle welfare in dairy and livestock farms.

Power Tiller

Powered by a reliable petrol engine, it delivers strong performance for tilling, weeding, and seedbed preparation. The machine features rugged wheels, adjustable handlebars, and sturdy blades that allow easy maneuverability and deep soil penetration even in tough conditions. With its fuel-efficient engine and low maintenance design, this tiller helps farmers save time and labor while achieving better crop yield through improved soil aeration and mixing.
